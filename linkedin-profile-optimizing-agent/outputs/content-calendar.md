# LinkedIn Content Calendar

## Weekly Content Strategy

Use this calendar to build visibility for AI Product Management, Senior Product Manager, Lead Product Manager, Platform Strategy, Product Growth, SaaS, Fintech, and GenAI roles.

| Day | Theme | Post Type | Draft Topic | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Monday | AI Product Management | Framework | Build vs Buy AI decisions | Ask product leaders how they evaluate AI investments |
| Tuesday | Product Strategy | Case study | How to prioritize AI roadmap bets | Invite comments from PMs |
| Wednesday | Growth | Metrics | AI success metrics beyond accuracy | Ask for measurement approaches |
| Thursday | Platform Strategy | Operating model | Turning AI features into platform capabilities | Invite product/platform discussion |
| Friday | Career Positioning | Lessons learned | Lessons from AI, SaaS, fintech, and digital products | Connect with recruiters and hiring managers |

### Takeaway

### CTA
