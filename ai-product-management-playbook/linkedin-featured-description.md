# LinkedIn Featured Description

AI Product Management Playbook by Saurabh Chawda, Lead Product Manager with 8+ years across AI products, GenAI, platform strategy, fintech, SaaS, digital payments, growth, and product-led revenue.

This playbook shares my framework for building AI products that move beyond demos and drive adoption, trust, revenue, and measurable business impact. It covers the AI product lifecycle, build vs buy decisions, RAG vs agent use cases, prioritization, success metrics, common mistakes, and selected product patterns from Logix Built Solutions, JoVE, and Mahindra Comviva.
