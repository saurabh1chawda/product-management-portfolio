## Slide 5: AI Product Lifecycle

Problem → Discovery → Validation → Experimentation → Scale

## Slide 6: Problem and Discovery

- Start with workflow, decision, or pain point
- Identify frequency, severity, current workaround, and business impact
- Define baseline metrics before solution design

## Slide 7: Validation and Experimentation

- Validate problem, AI fit, and business case
- Define hypothesis, success metric, guardrail metric, feedback loop, and decision rule

## Slide 8: Scale

## Slide 9: Build vs Buy AI Framework

- Build when AI creates durable product advantage
- Buy when capability is standard
- Use hybrid when speed and differentiation both matter

## Slide 10: RAG vs Agent Framework

- RAG: grounded knowledge and trusted answers
- Agents: multi-step execution across tools and systems
- Do not overbuild when a simpler workflow solves the problem

## Slide 11: AI Prioritization Framework

- Customer pain
- Business impact
- AI fit
- Data readiness
- Trust requirement
- Scalability

## Slide 12: AI Success Metrics Framework

Model Quality → Product Experience → User Adoption → Business Impact → Scalable Economics

## Slide 13: Common AI Product Mistakes

- Starting with the model instead of the problem
- Treating AI as a feature label
- Measuring accuracy but ignoring adoption
- Over-automating before trust exists
- Forgetting operational scale
- Building one-off AI features
